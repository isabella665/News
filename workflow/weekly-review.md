# Weekly Review — Monday Check-in Prep Template

**Cadence: Every Monday, before team check-in**

## Steps

1. Pull all flagged items from `ideas-bank/` for the past 7 days
2. Group by theme or urgency
3. Draft the short list below
4. Save as `weekly-reviews/YYYY-WNN.md` and bring to check-in

---
