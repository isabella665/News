## Inbox Setup

All alerts should route to a **dedicated inbox folder** (e.g. label/folder: `AI-News-Alerts`).

### Gmail filter (example)
- From: `googlealerts-noreply@google.com`
- Apply label: `AI-News-Alerts`
- Skip inbox: No (keep visible for morning scan)

### Review cadence
- Reviewed **every morning** as part of the daily scan
- Flag anything with a comms angle into the ideas bank

## Tips for Tuning Alerts

- Use **exact phrases** in quotes for multi-word terms (Google Alerts supports this)
- Set delivery to **"As-it-happens"** or **"Once a day"** depending on volume
- For high-volume terms (e.g. "OpenAI"), consider **"Only the best results"** to reduce noise
- Periodically audit: remove terms generating no useful signal, add emerging topics
