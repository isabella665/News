## Flag Threshold

Flag an item if it has at least one of:
- Relevance to AI governance, safety, or regulation
- A named org angle (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google DeepMind, policymakers)
- Potential reputational or narrative impact for the comms team
- Surprising public opinion data or survey findings
